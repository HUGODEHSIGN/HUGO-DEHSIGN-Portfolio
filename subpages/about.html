About Me

I'm Hugo Hsi, a designer with a passion for the technical side of creativity. I love exploring new design tools and automating processes to streamline the creative journey. My focus is on 2D and 3D animations, and I find relaxation and inspiration in crafting intricate origami.

In my previous role at Lookout, I created a wide array of marketing assets, including digital and print banners that captured attention, engaging motion graphics, and polished collateral materials. I also played a versatile role within the team, assisting with various tasks to ensure project success.

Armed with a BFA degree in Communication Design, I bring a strong foundation in graphic design to my work. My passion for origami adds a unique perspective to my creative process. Feel free to reach out at hhsi@hugodehsign.com if you'd like to connect or explore potential collaborations.
